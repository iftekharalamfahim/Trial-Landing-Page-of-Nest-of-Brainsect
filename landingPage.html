Trial of nest of Brainsect

31st January, 2025

After two weeks of simple learning and practicing I was able to write down a demo website called, Nest of Brainsect.

The basic idea was to design and create a responsive website, themed on 1940s newspaper. I am thinking about receating The Telegraph from 1940s (WWII era). I have seen some examples in google's news archive. More column on my inspiring websites and designs will be written later.

The motivation behind my idea was, basically if we try to analysis today's websites, they are the same like the newspaper or magazines from 18's & 19's century. I will try to make the websites completely unique.

This is just a trial and demo page where I tried to impliment some basics that I have learned so far. This is NOT near to the final version. It is expected that there will be a lot of bug and broken link in this demo. I hope to learn from those mistakes.
